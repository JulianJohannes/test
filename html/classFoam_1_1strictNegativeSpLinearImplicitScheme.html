Detailed Description

The class strictNegativeSpLinearImplicitScheme implements an implicit discretization of the source term that ensures contributions to the diagonal dominance of the resulting algebraic linear system.

The linearization constants are computed as

which ensures that the constant for all cells [2]. The resulting source term, written as

thus switches between explicit and implicit discretization cell by cell and depending on the sign of . This addresses the problem that unconditional implicit treatment can cause the solution to diverge. The reason is that a positive reduces the diagonal coefficient in the assembled linear system. This harms the diagonal dominance of the matrix, which may then lose its M-matrix and discrete stability property.
