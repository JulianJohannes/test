\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The class \mbox{\hyperlink{classFoam_1_1simpleLinearImplicitScheme}{simple\+Linear\+Implicit\+Scheme}} implements an implicit discretization of the source term in the most simplest way. 

\label{_Description}%


The linearization constants are calculated as \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} S_{simpleLinearImplicit,\,c} &= 0\\ S_{simpleLinearImplicit,\,p} &= f_{nl}(\psi^n), \end{aligned} \end{equation} resulting in the simple\+Linear\+Implicit source \begin{equation} S_{simpleLinearImplicit}(\psi^{n+1}) = f_{nl}(\psi^n)\psi^{n+1}_c. \end{equation}
